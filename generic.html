April 25, 2012-創建

峰群國際包裝科技

隨著各項包裝技術的日新月異，峰群國際包裝科技有限公司在全體員工努力之下也不斷地追求革新及精進本身技術升級，更在製作產品上針對環境及環保概念運用不斷配合政策提升品質,達成食品安全包裝及環保標準,將配合政府生產力4.0方向提升公司產能,效能,創造揚名於國際首屈一指的包裝指標；同時結合各種不同領域的產業升級取得資訊上,服務上迅速，共同發展出最大的效益，也發揮「取之社會、用之社會」的精神回饋給需要的人們。並在環保議題上盡一份心力，達到永續發展的目標。

With the rapid development of various packaging technologies, FengChiun International Packaging Technology Co., Ltd., with the efforts of all employees, is constantly pursuing innovation and improving its own technology upgrades. It also continues to cooperate with policies to improve quality in the production of products in accordance with the environment and eco-friendly concepts. Achieving food safety packaging and eco-friendly standards, will cooperate with the government's productivity 4.0 direction to enhance the company's production capacity and efficiency, and create world-renowned packaging indicators; meanwhile, it will combine information from various industrial upgrades in different fields to obtain information, provide rapid service and jointly develop the largest Benefit, but also give play to the spirit of "take society, use society" to give back to people in need. And make every effort on environmental issues to achieve the goal of sustainable development.
